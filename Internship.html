Meet Your Goals

We will help you explore and excel in areas that interest you and meet your personal and professional goals.

We allow our interns to experience the dynamics of the real workplace and thereby help them decide upon and shape their future careers.

Application Timeline:

Applications for the internship program open every January.
CV’s are screened to identify the most capable and suitable candidates and HR interviews are conducted in March.
Acceptance letters are sent during April, May and June.

Our Program

Begins At The Beginning Of July With A Four-Day Orientation Period:
Day 1: General Orientation including company history, culture, values, etc. In addition to a session for exploring career opportunities and brief about the Program Structure.
Day 2: Soft skills session and factory tour
Day 3: Selling Skills for Sales and Marketing Interns
Day 4: Product Knowledge for Sales and Marketing Interns
